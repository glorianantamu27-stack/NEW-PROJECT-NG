Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with your organization?

We offer various volunteering opportunities including event support, community outreach, administrative help, and specialized roles based on your skills. Please indicate your interest in volunteering when you contact us.

Do you provide legal assistance?

While we don't provide direct legal representation, we offer guidance on human rights issues, connect people with legal resources, and advocate for policy changes that protect rights at a systemic level.

Can you conduct workshops for our organization?

Yes! We regularly conduct human rights workshops for schools, community groups, and organizations. Contact us to discuss your specific needs and scheduling.

How is my donation used?

Donations support our educational programs, advocacy campaigns, community outreach, and operational costs. We're committed to transparency and provide annual reports on how funds are allocated.

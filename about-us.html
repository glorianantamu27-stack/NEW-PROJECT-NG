Our Story

The Right To Be Advocates was founded in 2015 by a diverse group of human rights activists, lawyers, educators, and community organizers who shared a common vision: creating a world where every person can live authentically and freely.

Our journey began in a small community center where we noticed a critical gap between human rights principles and people's understanding of how to exercise those rights in daily life. We started with workshops, community discussions, and advocacy training sessions.

Today, we've grown into an international movement with active chapters in over 20 countries, reaching thousands of people through our educational programs, advocacy campaigns, and community initiatives.
